INTRODUCTION

This report represents a synthesis of regional mapping and paleontologic information ob- tained by the various co-authors working in difkrent parts of the northern Appalachians and adjacent areas. Ayrton mapped the Port Daniel region of Gaspe Peninsula under the auspices of the Quebec Department of Natural Resources; he recognized the Weir Formation, its relationships to both the overlying Clem- ville Formation and the underlying Ordovician rocks, and found fossil localities of early Llandovery age in the area.
