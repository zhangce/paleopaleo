The presence of Spirigerina (Fospirigerina) praemarginalis in the Ellis Bay Formation (Boucot and Johnson, 1967) indicates that at least part of the unit is of Silurian rather than Ordovician age, as this species occurs elsewhere only in strata of early Llandovery age. Taken as a whole, the fauna of the Ellis Bay Formation as described by Twenhofel can be assigned as easily to the early Llandovery as to the Ordovician.
