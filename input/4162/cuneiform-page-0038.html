West of Washburn and Caribou, Maine, the lower member of the Perham Fornication, which consists of green argillite and shale with lenses of maroon and red shale and lenticular sedimentary manganese deposits, is generally in gradational or fault contact with the Carys Mills Formation IFig. 3). The lower member of the Perham Formation does not contain fossils that clearly establish its age, except for one locality of late Llandovery Cg to Cs age.
