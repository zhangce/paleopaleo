Berry (in Berry and Boucot, in press) has restudied graptolites from the overlying black shale of the lower member of the Ross Brook Formation in Antigonish County and finds that they are about British zone 18 (Mono- graptus cyphus) or zone 19 (Monograptus gregarious) in age (Elles and Wood, 1918).
