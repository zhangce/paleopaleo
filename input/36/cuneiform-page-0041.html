The results provide a detailed sampling of the corals from the formation in this area and a reconnaissance collection of other fossils. Except as noted, the specimens are deposited in the Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County, Invertebrate Paleontology Sec- tion.

Field work was undertaken between August 4 and September 9, 198S, and August 4 and September 16, 1986. Part of the latter interval was devoted to investigation of the formation in Peru.
