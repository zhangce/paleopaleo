(b) The Bentham Grit Group (Lower)

The Bentham Grit Group, as defined in this paper, extends from the Upper Sabdenian to the top of the Kinderscoutian. For convenience, that part which falls within the Sabdenian and which consists only of the thin Clintsfield Grit, has been termed " Lower ", and that which occupies the whole of the Kinderscoutian Stage, and is by far the thicker part, has been termed " Upper ".
