Most of the crinoids studied here were collected from near-shore facies where most post-Missourian crinoid samples have been recorded. The paucity of offshore crinoid faunas in the post-Missourian strata is possibly an artifact of collec- tion bias as exposures of these units (e.g. Leavenworth Lime- stone and Big Springs Limestone) in the northern Midcontinent usually have not been available for detailed collecting.
