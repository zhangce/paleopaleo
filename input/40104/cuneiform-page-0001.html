The Conservation and Survey Division of the University of Nebraska-Lincoln is the agency designated by statute to investigate and interpret the geologically related natural resources of the state, to make available to the public the results of these investigations, and to assist in the develop- ment and conservation of these resources. It consists of informal program areas in geology, water, soils and remote sensing/geographic information systems.

The division is authorized to enter into agreements with federal, state and local agencies to engage in cooperative surveys and investigations in the state. Publications of the division and coop- erating agencies are available from the Conservation and Survey Division, 113 Nebraska Hall, University of Nebraska-Lincoln, Lincoln, Nebraska 68588-0517; by phone at: (402) 472-7523; or e- mail:csdsales@unl.edu. More information and a list of publications can be found on the Conserva- tion and Survey web site: http:/Lcsd.uni.cddu/ sd.htm.
