Halysites amplitubata [sic] possesses flat tubule tabulae char- acteristic of Halysites and should not be considered conspecific with the Gaspe C. amplitubulatus.

Among other species of Cystihalysites from the Silurian of the Baie des Chaleurs region, C. amplitubulatus is most similar to the somewhat older C. belledunensis Young and Noble (1987b), which has corallites of similar shape arranged in long ranks. It is distinguished from C. belledunensis by its larger, thicker walled corallites and much larger, more variable coenenchymal tubules; characters related to corallite arrangement cannot be compared because all specimens of C. amplitubulatus are fragmentary.
