These works are among the most valuable, for they provide clear descriptions and they are well. illustrated, but their use is limited by their provinciality. For example, three of the ten Henryhouse genera described herein are un- known from Czechoslovakia, and three of the re- mainder are the cosmopolitan Proetus, Culymene, and Oalmwsites.
