An initial sequence of 21 UAs (including ammonite data) was determined for the Pliensbachian and Toarcian from Queen Charlotte Islands material only. This included hypothetical assemblages for the top of the Sinemurian (data from Carter et al., 1998) and for the base of the Aalenian (data from Carter et aL, 1988; Carter and Jal<obs, 1991) in order to extend the ranges of species that cross the range-chart boundaries. Based on ammonite data, an additional composite section covering the entire uppermost Sinemurian to lower Aalenian interval was constructed with QCI samples (see Appendix C). In the composite QCI section, data from Oregon (Yeh, 1987b; Nagai, 1990) were inserted to fill in the Lower Toarcian, which does not contain radiolarians in Queen Charlotte islands (see levels 21 and 22 in the QCI composite section, Appendix C).
