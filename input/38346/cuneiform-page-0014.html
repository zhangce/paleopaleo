The studied samples are from the lower part of the Musallah Formation as defined by Bechennec (1987). The succession consists of varicoloured bedded radiolarian chert interstratified locally with resedimented limestones and is assigned to the Lower Pliensbachian to Middle-Upper Toarcian according to the current radiolarian dating. No age-diagnostic fossils other than radiolarians are associated. The list of samples and superpositions is given in Gorican et al. (2006).
