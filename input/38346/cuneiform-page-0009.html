Radiolarian collections from the Ghost Creek Formation are illustrated on section 7 (Whiteaves Bay), section 8 (Maude Island) and section 10 (Rennell Junction). The Ghost Creek Formation is Lower Pliensbachian and contains ammonites of the Imlayi and Whiteavesi zones (Smith et al., 1988; Smith and Tipper, 1996).

Z.1.1.3. Fannin Formation. The Fannin Formation attains a total thickness of approximately 110m and rests conformably on the Ghost Creek Formation. It is a coarsening upward unit exposed along shoreline, in creek banks and quarries on central Graham Island, in the Skidegate Inlet area and as far south as Louise Island.
