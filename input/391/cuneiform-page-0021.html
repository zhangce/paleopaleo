Gaulocrinus bordeni is distinct from other Gaulocrinus species by having thin, flat aboral cup plates. Specimens of this species from Button Mold Knob have typically been referred to G. trautscholdi rather than to G. bordeni. The New Providence Shale Member specimens from north-central Kentucky are con- specific with those from the Fort Payne Formation of south- central Kentucky, and they are all referable to G. bordeni.
