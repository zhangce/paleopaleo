A single, moderately well preserved Fort Payne specimen is consistent with 8'. spinosulus, except that the nature of the basals and surface plate sculpturing is unknown and ra- mule branch interval varies from two to four. In con- trast, the middle column is heteromorphic with nodals sub- stantially higher and wider than internodals. More internodals are added distally. The preservation of the column is insufFicient to allow determination of the internodal pattern of insertion along the middle column or the pattern of columnal types within internodes.
