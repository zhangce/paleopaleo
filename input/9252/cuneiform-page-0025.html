5.2. Late Permian climate and reef developments

According to Flugel and Reinhardt (1989), Permian reefs are differentiated into the follow- ing five types: (1) Palaeoapiysina reefs, (2) phyl- loid algal reefs, (3) stromatolite reefs, (4) Tubi phytes alga-l crust reefs, and (S) calcisponge reefs. Among them, the Late Permian (including "Mid- dle" Permian) reefs are dominated by (4) or (5), or both in association, with a minor member of (3).
