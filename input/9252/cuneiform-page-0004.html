The Permian strata occupy the main part of the Palaeozoic outcrop of the South Kitakarni Terrane. They are widely distributed throughout the terrane, lying on the Carboniferous with a regional unconformity, and overlain by the Trias- sic with a minor unconformity. The Permian rocks consist mainly of shallow marine shelf deposits, including terrestrial and deeper shelf to slope deposits.
