As a whole, the limestone section changes from grainstone in the lower part to wackestone in the upper part, suggesting an environmental transition from high-energy to low-energy up- wards. The sedimentary facies succession grades upward from sand shoal, through reef and la- goon, to shelf to slope facies, as described below in detail. This succession suggests that the car- bonate bank of the Iwaizaki Limestone was pro- gressively drowned during a general subsidence

episode.
