6. Taphonomy

The Cornet fossils show signs of scavenging, little evidence for weathering, but strong indic- ations of winnowing and post-depositional distor- tion. The fossils are all disarticulated, except for a pair of ornithopod vertebrae found in association (MTCO 69S-5).
