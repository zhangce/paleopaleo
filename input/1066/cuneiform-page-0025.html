Repenning (1976) reported two sea otter specimens, here referred to E. terraenovae, from the Etchegoin Formation at North Dome in the Kettleman Hills of central California. A lower jaw fragment (UCMP 32970) was collected from UCMP locality V73634 be- tween the Pseudocardi um and Macoma zones in the middle part of the Etchegoin Forma- tion. According to Repenning (1976), a few Pliohippus teeth were also collected from this locality which indicated a Hemphillian age for the fauna.
