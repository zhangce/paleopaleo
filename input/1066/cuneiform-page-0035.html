Either of these dispersal hypotheses for En- hydritherium is possible given the present state of our knowledge. The presumed coastal marine ecology of Enhydritherium terrae- novae, the earliest appearance of this species in Florida, and the complete absence of En- hydritherium in Asia would seem to favor the North Atlantic dispersal route.
