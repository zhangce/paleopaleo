These studies suggest that the Upper Bone Valley fauna is of latest Hemp- hillian age (ca. 4.5-5 Ma). Bone Valley taxa characteristic of the late Hemphillian are Agriotherium, Plesiogulo, Cervidae, Rhyn- chotherium, Hexameryx, and advanced species of Osteoborus, Gomphotherium, Pseudhipparion, Xeohipparion, Xannippus and Dinohippus. All of the taxa listed are known from the Payne Creek, Palmetto and Fort Green mines.
