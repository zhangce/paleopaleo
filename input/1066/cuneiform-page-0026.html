Based on this date and the great strati- graphic separation between the dated tuff and the Etchegoin strata from which the sea otter fossils were collected, Repenning (1976) re- garded these specimens as late Miocene in age. Repenning and Tedford (1977) discussed several otarioid pinnipeds from this same general stratigraphic interval, which they considered to be 5-6 Ma old.
