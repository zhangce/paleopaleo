Infraorder: Bibionomorpha HENNIG, 1973

The infraorder Bibionomorpha became more uniform and relations between its families more evident after separating the infraorders Axymyiomorpha and Anisopodomorpha. Recent families included in this infraorder are grouped in superfamilies Bibionoidea, Sciaroidea and Scatopsidoi- dea. Fossil families are: Protopleciidae, Pleciofungivoridae, Pleciomimidae and Mesosciophilidae. The oldest Bibionomorpha are known since the Lower Jurassic (ANSORGE 1996).
