Unit 5 is the highest unit exposed in the Te Miko section. It is composed of 3 m of friable soft sandstone with occasional horizontal Ophiomorpha burrows and fragmentary megafossils. The upper part of this unit is more heavily burrowed than its basal part, and the burrows are cemented in some places by limonite. Interspersed between the burrows are concretions con- taining entire specimens of echinoids, I.yreidus bennetti, and the nautiloid cephalopod, Aturia sp.
