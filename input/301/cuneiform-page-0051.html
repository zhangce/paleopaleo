Extant Xotomithrax are known only from temperate habitats (Griffin, 1966b) in the vicinity of Australia, New Zealand, and Norfolk Island. A closely related form, Ageitomaia baeckstroemi (Balss) (=Paramithrax baeckstroemi) has previously (Griffin, 1963) been referred to Not omi thrax but is now thought to belong to a distinct genus (Griffin and Tranter, 1986).
