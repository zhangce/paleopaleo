However, the zigzag sculptural pattern of the precordilleran material deviates considerably from the reg- ular, concentric pattern of known species of the genus. Among palaeotaxodontids, ornamentation has been employed as a key feature in systematic differentiation, e.g., Acila Adams and Ad- ams, Veteranella Patte, Tindaria (Deminucula) Iredale, and (Pectinucula) Quenstedt (McAlester, 1969; Puri, 1969). Shell sculpture is not only a taxonomic character but it also has func- tional significance. Therefore, distinction is not only morpho- logical but reflects behavioral dissimilarities.
