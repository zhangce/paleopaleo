Discussion. â€” The material described above closely resembles P. radiata described and figured by Castellaro (1966, p. 125). Similarities include the size, shape, position of the umbo, sub- umbonal sinus, and ornamentation. Castellaro pointed out the radial costae (15 in number) are restricted to the central portion of the shell flanks. However, well-preserved specimens show additional, weak radial costae on the anterior part of the valve, which are only occasionally preserved because of their weakness. The differences in the total number of costae between the Pre- cordilleran material and that of Castellaro is a result of pres- ervation conditions rather than specific diff'erences.
