Hypostome and librigena not known.

Pygidium semielliptical; length about one-half greatest width. Axis prominent, broad, broadly curved posterior end, terminates well before inner edge of border, strong postaxial ridge. Two axial ring furrows present; anterior furrow shallow; posterior furrow faint, incomplete. Articulating half-ring prominent. Pleu- ral field very narrow; width between one-half and two-thirds width of axis, crossed by two distinct pleural furrows and one faint interpleural furrow. Border well defined, gently convex. Single short, posteriorly directed spine is extension of anterior pleural band. Second poorly developed border spine may be present opposite posterior pleural furrow. Surface of exoskeleton smooth, border of exfoliated specimens appears pitted.
