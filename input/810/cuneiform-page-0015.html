Librigena narrow, lateral and posterior border gently convex. Lateral border furrow broad, shallow to absent posteriorly. Oc- ular platform expanded slightly posteriorly. Lateral margin gent- ly curved. Length of genal spine slightly more than twice length of posterior section of facial suture.

Hypostome and rostral plate fused. Moderately convex trans- versely and sagittally. Anterior lobe clearly differentiated from small posterior lobe. Anterior part of lateral border strongly curved ventrally. Posterior wings prominent. Surface granular; distinct terrace ridges on anterior border and posterior margin.

Pygidium, exclusive of marginal spines, subquadrate; length about equal to width. Axis prominent, strongly convex trans- versely, posterior quarter nearly Hat across crest, tapered poste- riorly, sharply rounded at posterior end; short, moderately de- veloped postaxial ridge reaches to posterior margin. Three dis- tinct, straight ring furrows present behind articulating ring furrow; fourth and fifth furrows faint, incomplete. Anterior ring furrows and articulating ring furrow extend into moderately deep pits at sides of axis. SmaB, poorly to moderately developed me- dian nodes present on anterior three rings, strongest development on smaller specimens ((4 mm in length). Pleural region nearly horizontal, tapered posteriorly; width slightly less than one-half width of axis, crossed by three or four, broad pleural furrows; fourth pleural furrow present only on smaller specimens ((4 mm in length). Termination of pleural furrows distinguishes be- ginning of border. Lateral margin expands posterolaterally into pair of long, straight, slender marginal spines. Marginal spines moderately to strongly divergent. Posterior margin between mar- ginal spines strongly curved posteriorly; moderately to strongly depressed behind axis. Surface partly to fully covered with fine granules.
