A strong case could be made for phyletic gradualism for the A/bertellina-Albertelloides clan in the Pioche Shale. The ranges of the closely related species, A/bertellina aspinosa Palmer (in Palmer and Halley, 1979), Albertelloides kitai, Albertelloides rectimarginatus Palmer (in Palmer and Halley, 1979), and A. cf. rectimarginatus (described below) do not overlap, although they occur in the same facies.
