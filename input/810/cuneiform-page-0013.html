Both K. crassinucha and the species described here are characterized by a cranidium with an oval shaped glabella and narrow fixi- gena, which are features also shared by some Russian species of Kooteniella (Suvorova, 1964). Sundberg (1994), who has studied species of Kootenia from the Ehmaniella Biozone, has suggested the probability that the genus has been oversplit. The Kootenia complex currently consists of such a large number of forms that a thorough evaluation is needed in order to determine the amount of intraspecific variation.
