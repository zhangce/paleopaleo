FACIES EFFECTS

Megafloral composition at sites in the lignitic strata is strongly influenced by facies (Table 1 and Figure 5). These effects are seen on both a local and a sub- regional scale. Floras from channel deposits tend to be relatively homogeneous, and to be made up of relatively few, durable plant remains such as leathery leaves and fruits or nuts.
