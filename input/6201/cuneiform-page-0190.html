This diversity is similar to that in the Bitter Creek area of the Washakie Basin (Savage, Waters, and Hutchison, 1972). A congruent sequence of the earliest occurrences of Echmatemys, Baptemys and Hadrianus in both sections supports an hypothesis that these taxa represent immigrational events of regional utility.
