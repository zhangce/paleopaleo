The Gastropoda of the Castle Hayne fauna belong to 17 families and 22 genera. They are preserved almost entirely as casts and their specific identification has been,made with great caution. Of the 36 species recognized, 5 are new and 23 have received only generic determination. The Gastropoda, with the exception of 2 species, are confined to the Wilmington and Rocky Point localities.
