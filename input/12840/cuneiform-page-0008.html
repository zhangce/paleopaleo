Field work in 1992 was concentrated at the northern plunge of the Zinda Pir anticlinorium, par- ticularly in the Domanda Formation of Bozmar Nadi (Fig. 1), and in the Drazinda Formation near Satta (north of Sangarh Naia, north of an area pros- pected by West et al., 1991; see also Case and West,

1991, and Nolf, 1991). Field work in 1994 was con- centrated at the southern plunge of the Zinda Pir anticlinorium, particularly in the Domanda Forma- tion of Takra Daab (Fig. 1). The 1994 field work yielded some ten cetacean skulls or partial skulls, including several with good associated postcranial remains.
