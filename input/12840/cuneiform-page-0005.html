The letter (reproduced in Fig. 2) men- tions Latrunidhan and Chharat in northern Punjab and Rakhi Gaj and Safed Tobah (or Toba) in the Sulaiman Range, and, citing Eames, implies that the lower Chocolate Clays are "definitely fresh-water,'" and states that these are "beautifully exposed over literally hundreds of miles in easily accessible coun- try" where "very rich collections could be made."
