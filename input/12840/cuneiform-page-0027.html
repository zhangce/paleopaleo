The first of the Sulaiman faunas, the Habib Rahi fauna, is rich in well-preserved fish fossils (Rahman and Dunkle, 1966), and has yielded a well-pre- served, partially articulated skeleton of a new pro- tocetid archaeocete (Gingerich, 1991; not yet stud- ied in detail), that appears to be intermediate in morphology between earlier Pakicetus and later protocetids.
