The sediment at the Wilcox County locality is much finer grained, consisting of a slightly sandy and glauconitic, sticky clay with calcitic caliche, very fine gypsum crystals, and rare, dark-gray clasts with sepulid worm encrustations. The abundant benthic foraminifer fauna is dominated by species of Xodosaria, Dentalina, and I-enticulina, and the ostracode fauna consists primarily of smooth, nonalate bairdiids and cytherellids.
