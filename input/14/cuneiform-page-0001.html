INTRODUCTION HE NINGmA region is in the eastern part of northwest China.

Namurian rocks and associated faunas are widely distrib- uted through the region. Localities of particular interest to this study are the Zhongning, Zhongwei, Tongxan, Hulusitai and Shitanjing areas on the northern side of Mt. Helanshan (Figure I). The Ningxia area has received much attention from geolo- gists recently and extensive collections have been made of am- monoids, conodonts, brachiopods, corals and plants in addition to gastropods. The Tsingyuan Formation has been divided into Lower, Middle and Upper Members.
