Although we include it for completeness, our best guess is that Sailestes quadrans is a metatherian.

Biogeographic and biochronologic considerations

Paranyctoides is known from the late Turonian through possibly the Santonian of Middle Asia, while it, along with Gallolestes are known from the early and middle Campanian of North America. Turonian-aged therians have been reported from North America (Cifelli 1990b), but nothing referable to Paranyctoides or Gallolestes has been described.
